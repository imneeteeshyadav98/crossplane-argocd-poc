Thanks for using rds-guard!

This chart installs:
- Crossplane provider-aws (if enabled)
- RBAC so Kyverno can watch Crossplane RDSInstances
- A Kyverno policy to block public RDS or no deletion protection (deny rule by default)
- A sample RDSInstance (values.rds.*)

To test the policy, keep:
  rds.forProvider.publiclyAccessible: true
  rds.forProvider.deletionProtection: false
and Kyverno should deny the resource (with validationFailureAction=Enforce).
